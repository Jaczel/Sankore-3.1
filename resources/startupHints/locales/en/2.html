The teacher guide is a mutual resource that contains useful information for teachers.

You can find it by clicking on the teacher hat located on drawer on the left side of the screen.

This teacher guide offers you the ability to :

  • Insert author and copyright information.

  • Provide session information (title, lesson objectives, student or teacher activities...).

  • Describe the resource (school level, curriculum, keywords, license of the resource...).

  • Provide lesson instructions for the given resource.

  • Store additional or optional elements (image, sound, videos, application, web link, …) as well as the corrected exercises where relevant.

The aim of The Teacher Guide is to provide an overview of the activity.

NB: The Teacher Guide information will not be accompanied when exporting an Open-Sankoré document to a PDF format. This feature will hopefully be included in future releases of Open-Sankoré.
